I don't care

I'll help you

I don't care 

Ill suffer a thousand years for so long

I don't care

I'll help you

I don't care

I'll cry a thousand tears if i'm wrong

I do care

That smile that faded

I do care

That moment created

I do care

I'll help you

I do care

I mean it,

I swear

I'll help you

I'm sure of it

I'll help you

I promised
